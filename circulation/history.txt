== Showing a member's circulation history
 
OpenBiblio stores information about copies that a member has checked out previously.  You can access this list
after you retrieve a member account.

include::retrieving.txt[]

=== Viewing the history

Click on the History button, near the top of the screen.

NOTE: Unlike some ILSs, OpenBiblio does not anonymize circulations after a certain period of time has elapsed.
Please know that this can be considered a member confidentiality issue in some jurisdictions.
