== Checking out materials

To check out materials, you must first open a member's account.

=== Retrieving a member's account.

. Click on Circulation on the left side of the screen.
. Enter a portion of the member's last name.  If you have member barcodes turned on, you may alternatively scan their barcode.

TIP: Anybody with administrator privileges can turn member barcodes on using Tools -> System Settings -> Use Member Barcodes.

=== Chcecking out an item

. Type or scan the item barcode into the Check Out field.
. If you would like to see what a member has checked out and when they are due, click "History".
